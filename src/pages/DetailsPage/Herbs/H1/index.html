Mint

Mentha Linn

About Plant

Mint, with its fresh aroma and distinctive flavor, is a prominent herbal plant in the culinary world and traditional medicine. Mint species, such as peppermint and spearmint, have dark green oval-shaped leaves that are toothed, with stems that grow quickly. The fresh scent of mint comes from the essential oils found in its leaves, giving it a refreshing menthol flavor. Mint is used in a variety of cuisines, beverages, and health products. The leaves can be used fresh or dried to add flavor to foods, beverages, and cakes. Besides its culinary value, mint is also known in traditional medicine to relieve digestive problems and provide a fresh effect on breathing. The plant thrives in moist soil, but needs to be watched out for as it can grow very quickly and become invasive. With its role in creating refreshing drinks like mojitos and mint tea, as well as its health benefits, mint continues to be a popular herbal plant around the world.
