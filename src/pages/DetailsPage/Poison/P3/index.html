Ficus elastica

About Plant

Ficus elastica, commonly known as the rubber tree or rubber plant, is a popular indoor plant appreciated for its robust nature and distinctive appearance. Native to Southeast Asia, this evergreen tree is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves that are typically dark green, although some varieties may have variegated patterns. While the Ficus elastica or rubber tree plant is generally considered safe for indoor growing, it is important to remember that the sap or latex found in this plant can cause skin irritation in some sensitized individuals. Therefore, it's best to wear gloves when tending to or pruning this plant, especially if one has sensitive skin. Also, keep in mind that this plant is toxic if consumed, both by humans and pets. Therefore, it is advisable to keep this plant out of the reach of small children and pets. While beauty and good aerial benefits are the main attractions of Ficus elastica, awareness of potential irritants and toxicity is essential to ensure safety and security at home or office.
